# Visual Bible

## Format

- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Tone: warm realist coastal drama
- Runtime target for MVP: 6 shots, 36-45 seconds
- Test render: opening shot only, 6 seconds

## Character Continuity

- Subject: 7-year-old Korean island child
- Wardrobe: slightly oversized yellow raincoat, navy school backpack, white socks, worn sneakers
- Props: small name tag on backpack, one hand holding a folded paper schedule
- Behavior: quiet, observant, nervous but brave

## Location

- Small island ferry pier at early morning
- Wet wooden dock, coiled rope, low concrete seawall, weathered ferry sign
- Calm gray-blue sea with faint ripples
- Distant school ferry approaching as a tiny shape, never dominant in the opening shot

## Light And Color

- Soft morning backlight from camera-left horizon
- Misty blue-gray sea, muted yellow raincoat as emotional focal point
- Skin tones natural, no glossy commercial grading
- Gentle haze, light wind

## Camera Grammar

- Restrained Korean indie-film pacing
- Low child-height camera
- Slow dolly-in or almost-still handheld drift
- Natural lens feel, around 35mm
- No fast cuts in the opening

## Texture

- Damp wood grain
- Slight salt haze in the air
- Soft rain residue on the raincoat
- Practical realism over polished fantasy

## Negative Visual Constraints

- No cartoon style
- No exaggerated cinematic orange/teal grade
- No crowded port
- No dramatic storm
- No adult face dominating the frame
- No text overlays or subtitles in generated footage
