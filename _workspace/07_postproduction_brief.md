# Postproduction Brief

Status: updated after s01 test render.

## Edit Rhythm

Open with 1.5 seconds of stillness before the tiny hand movement is visible. Let the cut breathe; avoid making the child's first school day feel like a trailer.

The completed s01 render can hold as the opening shot. If the final edit needs a slower emotional entrance, use the first two seconds under ambient sound before any music cue.

## Sound

- Low morning sea ambience.
- Soft dock creak.
- Distant ferry engine, almost subliminal.
- No sentimental piano in the opening shot; if music enters later, keep it sparse.

## Color

- Natural gray-blue coastal palette.
- Keep yellow raincoat warm but not neon.
- Avoid heavy contrast and orange/teal grading.

## Captions

No text in generated footage. If the final film needs captions, add them in edit, not generation.

## Rerender Pickup Needs

After the first render, decide whether to create a reference still from the selected frame for subsequent shots.

Use `_workspace/renders/s01_attempt2_midframe.jpg` as a continuity still candidate for s02/s03 prompt references.
